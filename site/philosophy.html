Our Philosophy

Personalized Care

We believe that personal care of our patients is of the utmost importance. Our staff takes the time to get to know you as a person, learns your history, and listens to your individual goals and concerns. Each session, which averages 60 to 80 minutes, begins with insightful questioning followed by a thorough examination of the body. Problem areas are addressed with an effective plan of care which will be tailored to the specific needs of the patient.


Wellness For Life

Our main focus is to minimize your pain and immediately improve your function and quality of life. Through a combination of our hands on treatments, tailored exercises, and your dedication to a positive resolution, improvements will be easily achieved. Our methods include treatments from the conventional to the newest cutting edge techniques. As you progress, we will adjust your treatments periodically to fit your body’s responses towards reaching your goals.

 

Making changes in the body to last is challenging because it requires a commitment to long-term wellness. We will educate you on how to permanently correct old postural patterns which cause muscle imbalances and replace them with more efficient ways to move. We will help by having thorough discussions on nutrition, diet, exercise progression, injury prevention, and stress management. By the end of your time with us, we will ensure that you are given the proper tools for self-management and maintenance for long lasting wellness in the home.
